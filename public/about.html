About Me

My name is Madilyn Metzler, but most people call me Maddy. I am a muslim woman born on October 2nd, 2004 and I am a Web Developer in training! I am taking a course by Dr. Angela Yu on Udemy.com to become a full-stack web developer and learn the in's and out's of web development.

I was born and raised in Arizona where the sun shines... constantly. Because of this, I prefer the indoors. Inside is where I can let my mind run free and create (and not get heatsickness). I've always loved art since I was a kid. Whether it be drawing, painting, reading, writing, photography, etc. I loved creating. I also loved watching and observing other's creations through books, movies, shows, music, or video games. As I grew up, I was unsure if I could pursue a secure career in such things so I settled in retail for a few years. I started to grow tired of not having anything to strive for and life felt bland. Until I discovered coding. My spouse was taking courses for Python and showed me all the things he had learned and created. Through him I found a new passion. I researched all the types of jobs one could pursue in tech and coding and discovered web development. Everything about it sounded perfect for me and, for the first time in a long time, I had something I passionately wanted to work towards. I am excited to learn more and eventually be able to begin a career developing websites.

If you haven't already, take a look at some of the projects I worked on while training. Don't take the contents too seriously! It's more of a showcase of what I've learned and what I am able to do thus far.
